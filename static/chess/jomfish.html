About Jomfish

Jomfish is a lightweight chess engine created as a personal project. It is written in Rust (more than 8.000 lines) and offers a unique perspective on chess AI development. While not as strong as Stockfish, it is a fun and competitive option for chess enthusiasts looking for something different.
